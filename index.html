Venn Diagram Study Strategy

My preparation strategy focuses on three competitive examinations: SSC CGL AAO, IMD Scientific Assistant, and RBI Grade B. Instead of studying each exam separately, I designed a strategy using a Venn Diagram approach.

This strategy helps identify overlapping subjects between exams such as Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, English, and General Awareness. By mastering these shared topics first, I can efficiently prepare for multiple exams simultaneously.

After completing the common topics, I will focus on specialized subjects such as Finance, Economics, Accounting, and Technical Subjects depending on each examination's requirement.
